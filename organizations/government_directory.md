# Kerala Government Departments, Agencies & Public Sector Undertakings

A comprehensive directory of key Kerala government departments, agencies, and
public sector undertakings with their official websites and verified social
media handles. This directory covers core governance, infrastructure, industry,
education, health, agriculture, environment, and law enforcement entities.

Last verified: April 2026

## Notes

- Entries marked with `--` indicate that no verified official account was found
  on that platform at the time of research.
- Some entities maintain multiple X (Twitter) accounts (e.g., KSRTC, KWA, MVD).
  Only the primary or most active official account is listed.
- The Coir Board is a central government body headquartered in Kochi; it is not
  a Kerala state entity but is included due to its deep connection to the
  Kerala coir industry.
- Website URLs are subject to change. The `.kerala.gov.in` and `.gov.in` domains
  are official government domains.
- Social media handles should be verified periodically as accounts may change or
  become inactive.
