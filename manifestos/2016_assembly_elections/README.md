# 2016 Assembly Election Manifestos

The 14th Kerala Legislative Assembly election was held on May 16, 2016.
LDF won 91 out of 140 seats, defeating the incumbent UDF government.
Pinarayi Vijayan became Chief Minister.

## LDF (Left Democratic Front)

- **File:** [ldf-mal.pdf](ldf-mal.pdf) (Malayalam, 72 pages)
- **Source:** [Wayback Machine](https://web.archive.org/web/2016/http://www.mmmani.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/LDF_Election_Manifesto.pdf)
- **English version:** [Scribd](https://www.scribd.com/document/488124864/kerala-ldf-manifesto-2016-english-version) (may require account)

The LDF manifesto contained approximately 600 proposals under the theme
"We Need a New Kerala". Key promises included:

- Comprehensive land reform and housing for all
- Agricultural modernization and food security
- Industrial development through KIIFB
- Universal healthcare and education
- Women's empowerment and gender justice
- Environmental protection and sustainable development
- Decentralization through local self-governance

## UDF (United Democratic Front)

- **File:** [udf-mal.pdf](udf-mal.pdf) (Malayalam, 64 pages)
- **Source:** [KeralaClick](https://keralaclick.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/udf-manifesto-2016.pdf)

Key themes included:

- Continuation of welfare programmes
- Infrastructure development
- Employment generation
- Agricultural and industrial growth
- Education and healthcare

## NDA (National Democratic Alliance / BJP)

- **File:** [nda-mal.pdf](nda-mal.pdf) (Malayalam, 28 pages)
- **Source:** [Wayback Machine / keralabjp.org](https://web.archive.org/web/20170203113727/http://keralabjp.org/download/vision-document/item/download/140_9dc5332c4802bba7bea8f92d55cd8f0d)

This is the NDA "വികസനരേഖ" (Vision Document) for the 2016 election, titled
"ദൈവത്തിന്റെ സ്വന്തം നാടിനെ നമ്മൾ വീണ്ടെടുക്കും" (We will reclaim God's Own
Country). The motto is "Directive Principles of State Policy is our Motto".
Key themes included:

- Development based on constitutional directive principles
- Good governance and anti-corruption
- Economic self-reliance
- Infrastructure and industrial development
- Social justice and welfare

## Election Result Summary

| Alliance | Seats Won |
|----------|-----------|
