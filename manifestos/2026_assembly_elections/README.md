# 2026 Assembly Election Manifestos

The 16th Kerala Legislative Assembly election is scheduled for April 9, 2026. Below are
the election manifestos from the three major political fronts.

## LDF (Left Democratic Front)

- **File:** [ldf-mal.pdf](ldf-mal.pdf) (Malayalam, 49 pages)
- **QR Code URLs:** [ldf-mal-qr-urls.txt](ldf-mal-qr-urls.txt)
- **Video Transcripts:** [ldf-mal-video-transcripts.md](ldf-mal-video-transcripts.md) (76 videos, Malayalam + English)
- **Video Metadata:** [ldf-mal-video-metadata.json](ldf-mal-video-metadata.json)

The manifesto PDF contains 70 QR codes linking to YouTube Shorts on
[ldfmanifesto2026.com](https://ldfmanifesto2026.com). The video transcripts include
both on-screen text (slideshow content) and speech, extracted using
the Google Gemini API.

This is the summary manifesto (പ്രകടന പത്രിക) containing a 60-point programme with
950 proposals summarized under the theme "നവകേരളം പൂർത്തീകരിക്കാൻ" (To complete
Nava Kerala). Key promises include:

- Welfare pension increase from Rs 2,000 to Rs 3,000
- Complete eradication of absolute poverty
- Employment for 60,000 people immediately; 20 lakh jobs for women over 5 years
- 50% women's workforce participation
- LIFE Mission 2.0 for universal housing
- SilverLine semi-high-speed rail, metro expansions
- Renewable energy capacity expansion to 5,000 MW
- Interest-free loans for youth entrepreneurs
- Kerala's first AIIMS-level medical research hospital

## UDF (United Democratic Front)

- **File:** [udf-mal.pdf](udf-mal.pdf) (Malayalam, 104 pages)

The UDF manifesto is titled "പുതുയുഗ കേരളത്തിന്" (For a New-Age Kerala) and covers
39 promise areas across 4 sections. Key themes include:

- Social security and equality
- Welfare schemes and healthcare
- Agriculture and rural development
- Education and employment
- Industry and tourism
- Infrastructure and transportation (KSRTC reform, roads, ports)
- Women's empowerment and child welfare
- Environmental protection and governance
- Secularism and communal harmony

## NDA (National Democratic Alliance / BJP)

- **File:** [nda-eng.pdf](nda-eng.pdf) (English, 120 pages)

The NDA manifesto is titled "Vikasita Keralam" (Developed Kerala) with the tagline
"A New Vision of Prosperity for All Malayalees" and the hashtag #PoliticsOfPerformance.
It covers the period 2026-2031. Key promises include:

- Quality of Life & Affordable Living (health insurance, AIIMS, free water supply, LPG subsidy, welfare pension enhancement)
- Infrastructure for Vikasita Keralam (high-speed rail, railway expansion, Vizhinjam port, metro connectivity)
- Investment & Jobs Revolution (employment-linked industrial rejuvenation, electronics industry)
- Free optional English medium education until Class 12
- Swachha Keralam (waste management)
- Drugs-free state and crime rate reduction
- AI-powered corruption-free governance
- SC, ST & OBC empowerment
- Mullaperiyar Dam safety, coastal protection, wildlife encounter solutions
- Modern cities and Atmanirbhar villages
