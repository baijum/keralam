# 2021 Assembly Election Manifestos

The 15th Kerala Legislative Assembly election was held on April 6, 2021, with
results declared on May 2, 2021. The incumbent LDF won a historic victory,
retaining power with 99 seats. Below are the election manifestos from the three
major political fronts with download links.

## LDF (Left Democratic Front)

- **File:** [ldf-mal.pdf](ldf-mal.pdf) (Malayalam, 23 pages)
- **Extracted Text:** [ldf-mal-text.md](ldf-mal-text.md)
- **Source:** [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/ldf-manifesto-2021)
- **English version:** [Scribd](https://www.scribd.com/document/613796031/Manifesto-CPIM-2021-ldf) (may require account)

### Key Promises
- 40 lakh new jobs for youth
- Pension for all housewives
- Rs 5,000 crore package for coastal area development
- Social security pension increase to Rs 2,500 in phases
- Minimum support price for rubber raised to Rs 250/kg
- Self-sufficiency in eggs, milk, and vegetables

## UDF (United Democratic Front)

- **File:** [udf-mal.pdf](udf-mal.pdf) (Malayalam, 60 pages)
- **Extracted Text:** [udf-mal-text.md](udf-mal-text.md)
- **Source:** [Jaihind TV](https://jaihindtv.in/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/UDF-Manifesto-2021.pdf)

### Key Promises
- Rs 2,000 monthly pension for homemakers
- 5 kg free rice to all white card holders
- 5 lakh homes for the poor (Nyay Yojna implementation)
- Special law to protect Sabarimala traditions
- Peace and Harmony department (Rajasthan model)
- 2-year age relaxation for mothers in government job exams

## NDA (National Democratic Alliance / BJP)

- **File:** [nda-mal.pdf](nda-mal.pdf) (Malayalam, 24 pages)
- **Extracted Text:** [nda-mal-text.md](nda-mal-text.md)
- **Source:** [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/scribdfull.com_nda-manifesto-2021)

### Key Promises
- Free laptops for high school students
- New legislation for Sabarimala
- Law against Love Jihad
- Employment guarantee for at least one person per family
- Campaign slogan: "Puthiya Keralam Modikkoppam" (New Kerala with Modi)

## Election Result Summary

| Alliance | Seats Won | Change |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| LDF      | 99        | +8     |
| UDF      | 41        | -6     |
| NDA      | 0         | -1     |

The LDF's victory marked the first time an alliance won consecutive terms in
Kerala since 1977. Pinarayi Vijayan became the first Chief Minister to be
re-elected after completing a full five-year term.
