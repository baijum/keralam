## 2025-26

- **[Budget in Brief](2025-26/budget-in-brief-eng.pdf)** (English, 138 pages)

Presented by Finance Minister K.N. Balagopal on February 7, 2025.
Total expenditure estimated at Rs 2,00,354 crore. Fiscal deficit
targeted at 3.2% of GSDP.
