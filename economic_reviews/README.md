# Kerala Economic Review

Annual economic reviews published by the Kerala State Planning Board. These are
comprehensive publications covering all aspects of Kerala's economy, development
indicators, and sectoral performance.

Source: [spb.kerala.gov.in](https://spb.kerala.gov.in/) |
[Economic Review Archive](https://spb.kerala.gov.in/en/economic-review-archive)

## 2025

Published January 2026.

- **[Volume 1](2025/vol1-eng.pdf)** (English, 690 pages) — macroeconomic overview, sectoral analysis
- **[Volume 2](2025/vol2-eng.pdf)** (English, 540 pages) — statistical appendix

Key highlights: Fiscal deficit at 3.02% of GSDP in 2023-24. Tech startups
grew from 4,681 to 6,227 in 2024-25.

## 2024

Published February 2025.

- **[Volume 1](2024/vol1-eng.pdf)** (English, 692 pages) — macroeconomic overview, sectoral analysis
- **[Volume 2](2024/vol2-eng.pdf)** (English, 510 pages) — statistical appendix

Key highlights: GSDP growth of 6% at constant prices in 2023-24.
Per capita GSDP Rs 1,76,072. Kerala ranked first in NITI Aayog SDG Index.
