# 2021 Kerala Legislative Assembly Election Results

**Election Date:** 6 April 2021
**Results Declared:** 2 May 2021
**Total Constituencies:** 140 (14 SC-reserved, 2 ST-reserved, 124 General)
**Voter Turnout:** 74.02%

The 2021 election was historic: the Left Democratic Front (LDF) became the first
alliance to win consecutive terms in Kerala since 1977.

## Notable Results

- **Highest victory margin:** K. K. Shailaja (CPI(M)), Mattannur -- 60,963 votes
- **Lowest victory margin:** Najeeb Kanthapuram (IUML), Perinthalmanna -- 38 votes
- **Chief Minister:** Pinarayi Vijayan (CPI(M)), won from Dharmadom with a margin of 50,123
- **Leader of Opposition:** V. D. Satheesan (INC), won from Paravur with a margin of 21,301
- **Nemom:** V. Sivankutty (CPI(M)) won the constituency back from BJP, which had won it in 2016 (the only BJP seat that term)
